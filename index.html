What is Glassmorphism?

Glassmorphism is a design trend that creates a frosted glass effect using translucency, background blur, and subtle borders. It adds depth and visual hierarchy to interfaces while maintaining a modern, elegant appearance.

This design approach creates a sense of depth and layering that mimics real-world materials, making digital interfaces feel more tangible and engaging.
